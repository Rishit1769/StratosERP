📊 1. Academic Management & Assessment

The Subject Incharge manages the granular academic data and performance tracking for their assigned course modules.  

    Marks Management: Full authority to input and modify internal mid-term and end-semester marks.  

    Supplementary (Suppli) Logic: Independent entry of supplementary marks that automatically updates student dashboards upon saving.  

    Interactive Performance Heatmaps: Access to question-level analytics for exams, providing a visual heatmap to identify specific syllabus topics where students are struggling.  

    Subject Analytics: Access to subject-level performance metrics and score distribution charts to monitor overall class progress.  

💬 2. Classroom Operations & AI Integration

Faculty utilize integrated AI to automate administrative tasks and enhance lecture delivery.  

    Smart Attendance Module: Mark attendance using a session-linked digital roster that generates automatically based on the active timetable slot and system clock.  

    Bulk Toggle Interface: Utilize a rapid marking system with "Default Present" logic to minimize classroom disruption.  

    Threshold Monitoring: System automatically flags students falling below the 75% attendance threshold for administrative review.  

    Syllabus Pacing Analyzer: Utilize Gemini AI to compare logged execution logs against the master syllabus PDF to calculate real-time pacing status.  

🔄 3. Execution & Triage

The Subject Incharge ensures the delivery of the curriculum and resolves academic disputes.  

    Lecture Execution Logs: Mandatory post-lecture logging of exact syllabus topics and any additional topics taught during the session.  

    Grievance Resolution: Serve as the primary authority for resolving AI-routed grievances regarding academic marks disputes.  

    Teacher Guardian Assistant: Input subject data into Gemini AI to auto-generate "Areas of Improvement" reports for parent-teacher meetings.  

    Material Distribution: Upload class notes and study PDFs directly to MinIO object storage for student access.  

🔑 4. Operational Context

Every Subject Incharge operates within a strict role-based framework to ensure data security and accountability.  

    Multi-Designation: Faculty can simultaneously hold roles as a Subject Incharge, Class Incharge, and Teacher Guardian (TG).  

    Data Scope: Rights are strictly restricted to the specific subjects and classes assigned by the HOD for the current active semester.  

    Active Semester Context: All actions are governed by the global semester configuration set by the System Admin.
