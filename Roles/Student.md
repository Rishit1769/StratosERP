📊 1. Academic Dashboard & Management

The student interface provides a centralized hub for real-time academic tracking and schedule management.  

    Core Dashboard: View personal marks, attendance percentages, fee status, and AICTE points.  

    Subject Visibility: Active subjects for the current semester are displayed alongside any carry-forward records.  

    Backlog Tracking: Subjects failed in previous semesters are automatically displayed with a [KT] (Keep Term) or [SUPPLI] (Supplementary) badge until cleared.  

    Live Faculty Locator: Students can locate teachers in real-time by cross-referencing the master timetable with the system clock.  

    Schedule Management: Access to a full weekly timetable and a granular daily breakdown of lectures.  

💬 2. Communication & Campus Resources

Students utilize integrated AI and cloud storage tools for institutional communication and material access.  

    Grievance Portal: Submit structured complaints with evidence; Gemini AI analyzes the severity and routes the ticket to the appropriate authority.  

    AI-Enhanced Notice Board: Receive institutional broadcasts automatically tagged by Gemini AI with filters like [URGENT] or [HACKATHON].  

    Study Materials: Securely access and download class notes and PDFs distributed via MinIO object storage.  

🔄 3. Lifecycle & Progression

The system manages the student's status automatically based on academic milestones.  

    Year Labeling: Students are assigned a label (1st Year through 4th Year) based on the number of semesters completed.  

    Batch Progression: During semester transitions, the system re-computes the academic year label based on the admission batch.  

    Alumni Transition: Upon completion of Semester 8, students are moved to the Alumni section; active features like timetables are disabled, but read-only access to transcripts remains.  

    KT Protection: Students with uncleared backlogs are not auto-progressed to Alumni status until all subjects are cleared.  

🔑 4. Identity Standards

Every student is strictly identified using a unified protocol to ensure institutional reliability.  

    Institutional Email: The primary identifier for system access.  

    Standardized UID: A unique string formatted as: [StartYear]-[ClassDivision RollNo]-[EndYear].
